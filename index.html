Get Your Score

WHAT IS THIS?

BeeHappy lets you calculate how happy you are! Get your Happy Score and compare it to others in your area and across the country. Fill out the questions in our survey and BeeHappy will use your answers as well as a number of different data sets to come up with your own unique Happy Score. The formula for your happy score is similar to the formula for deriving Gross National Happiness.
