ARE YOU HAPPY?

Gross National Happiness is a way of measuring one aspect of a nation’s success overlooked by Gross Domestic Product, the traditional measurement used for comparing nations. This app is a step towards putting this measurement in place in Canada.

Gross National Happiness is currently used in Bhutan and has also been used in a number of other localities. For more info check out the Gross National Happiness Website.

The more people that participate, the better the measurement and outcome will be. So please help by using the app and finding out about how happy you are. We are currently using a limited number of data sets, as a proof of concept. If you would like to see this app expanded, let us know and we will continue working on it. Please suggest any data sets or questions you would like to see incorporated.

The intent is that, in time, this information could be used to help the government make policy decisions which would lead to a happier population.
